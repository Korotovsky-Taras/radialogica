SciMoCa™™ leverages the power of Monte Carlo for independent dose calculation and plan QA developed in collaboration with Prof. Dr. Markus Alber. SciMoCa combines the accuracy of Monte-Carlo dose calculations and the precision of custom beam models for robust dose verification and plan quality evaluation.

Benefits

Accuracy - a best-in-class Monte Carlo dose calculation engine with a prominent academic pedigree that delivers validated accuracy with fast calculation times

Precision - custom beam models to maximize calculation integrity with support for Varian, Elekta, Accuray, Tomo Therapy, ZAP-X and and Siemans delivery devices

Analysis - a comprehensive suite of dose analysis and reporting tools along with workflow automation to streamline plan review and approval

Deployment - integrates seamlessly within your IT infrastructure with no proprietary hardware required

Features
  • photon and electron Monte Carlo dose calculation
  • machine-specific, generic, and golden beam data models
  • comparative plan quality metrics
  • comparative DVH
  • 3D gamma analysis
  • image slice viewer
  • beam data table
  • plan approval and reporting

fullAccess'™ is a fully integrated radiotherapy data display, communication and analysis platform that enables clinical across the continuum of care to access, analyze and share RT data.

fullAccess supports flexible viewing of multi-modality diagnostic images and multi-vendor treatment plans extraction, analysis and benchmarking of plan quality metrics and sharing of RT data between facilities and caregivers for clinical collaboration.

Capabilities

View - isualize images and treatment plans for all major treatment modalities, delivery devices and planning systems from a single desktop application

Analyze - evaluate plan quality metrics with user-defined templates and benchmark performance against historic results

Annotate - add clinical commentary in the plan that can be reviewed and responded to by members of the treatment team

Package - bundle treatment plans, annotations, and file attachments into a single file with an embedded RT viewer

Share - distribute radiotherapy treatment plans and other files inside and outside the clinic, including patients and referral partners

Applications
  • plan review
  • remote review
  • peer review
  • plan quality benchmarking
  • case conference
  • referral partners
  • training and education
  • permanent and portable patient records
