Radiotherapy Quality Management

Radialogica is a healthtech company focused on radiation oncology for cancer patients. We provide tools, evidence and insight to manage the quality and safety of radiation treatment. Our goal is to enhance treatment quality improve patient outcomes and reduce the burden of cancer survivorship.

Our Mandate

Radialogica's work is centered around quality in radiation oncology how we define, measure, and manage treatment quality for cancer patients for the benefit of cancer patients and other stakeholders invested in their care.

Our Field

Radiation therapy is a proven and effective form of treatment for many types of cancer. According to the American Society for Radiation Oncology (ASTRO), nearly two-thirds of cancer patients in the United States, and over half of cancer patients worldwide, will receive radiation during their treatment.

Despite the high degree of computerization and automation that has come to define modern radiation oncology, there continues to exist significant variability in treatment planning and delivery. This variability can have direct consequences in terms of tumor control and toxicity.

The inherent complexity of radiotherapy treatment requires rigorous quality assurance to ensure safe and effective care. The evaluation of treatment quality can be performed prior to the start of treatment, presenting an opportunity for real-time prospective review and potential intervention.

Our Business

Radialogica provides analytics services and software tools to evaluate and manage RT treatment quality and enhance outcomes for cancer patients.
CLINICAL TRIALS SERVICES

RADIOTHERAPY QUALITY MANAGEMENT FOR ONCOLOGY TRIALS

Radialogica partners with study sponsors and CROs to manage the quality of radiation therapy in clinical trials. We work with big pharma, emerging biotech companies and medical device manufacturers to design and implement RT QA programs that anticipate and address the unique clinical and operational requirements for trials that involve either on-study or prior RT. We leverage our deep clinical experience, proprietary software tools, and a technology infrastructure purpose-built for RT data acquisition and analysis to flexibly support the needs of small phase I studies through large, complex, global pivotal trials. our study services span the development of RT treatment guidelines; collection and aggregation of RT data; extraction and evaluation of dosimetric parameters and other clinical quality measures; and comprehensive QA programs with prospective or retrospective case reviews and reporting.
